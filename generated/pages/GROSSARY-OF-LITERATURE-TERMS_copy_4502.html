correct, straight, perfect

spelling and sound at the beginning of the base to which the prefix is affixed eg

move away from concrete meaning; a feature of written texts; in narrative writing taking
the form of analogy used to explore the human condition, in factual and analytical writing
taking the form of grammatical metaphor and nominalisation

love, greed, happiness, height, heat;
