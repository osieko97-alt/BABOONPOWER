There are two principles of visual arts nam ely:  
1. Principle of dominance.  
2. Principle of proportion.  
 
1. Principle of dominance(emphasis) in Visual Arts.  
ÔÅ∂ Dominance is when a single element is so large and/or different compared to the rest of the design 
that it dominates. It grabs your attention.   
ÔÅ∂ It contributes to organic unity by emphasizing the fact that there is one main feature and that other

2. Principle of Proportion in Visual arts.
