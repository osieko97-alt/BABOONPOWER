Application Form


Qualification
1.   Each applicant must be in the last year of primary school, registered to sit for their KCPE exams in November 2018.2.  Applicants must be at least 13 and not more than 15 years of age at the time they are expected to join Form 1 in
     January 2019.
3.  We accept applications from all 47 Counties.4.  The Academy is an inclusive learning environment and students with mobility disabilities are encouraged to apply.
Instructions
1.   Please fill out the application form for 2018 ONLY, as clearly and as completely as possible. Where information is     absolutely not available, please mark (NOT AVAILABLE). However, remember that the more information you make      available to us the better it is for us to evaluate your application. Forms from previous years WILL NOT be accepted.2.  Attach copies of the following documents, failure to which your application WILL NOT be considered;
     a)  Birth Certificate
     b)  Parent/Guardian ID     c)  Parent Death Certificate, where applicable     d)  Academic reports for the LAST 3 YEARS signed and stamped by school official
     e)  Co-curricular activity certificates
     f)   Evidence of leadership     g)  Payslip of Parent/Guardian if employed     h)  Any other document deemed relevant4.  Note that admission into the M-PESA Foundation Academy will be based on an independent application process
     separate from the national Form 1 application and selections process.
5.  Write your first and last name in capital letters at the top of each page in case the pages detach and as it appears on     your Birth Certificate.6.  Only complete applications will be considered. 
7.   All essays MUST be written in the spaces provided within the form and filled by the student applying for the         
     You are NOT  allowed to insert any extra pages.
8.  Applications should be enclosed in a sealed envelope during submission.
9.  Online applications are encouraged. Ensure the application is scanned and attached as a single PDF document.
10. Please retain a copy in case it is lost in transit.  Note that only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.
11. Any violation of the above will lead to automatic disqualification.
